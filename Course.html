Library

Library has an extensive collection of books, reference materials for satisfying the academic and research needs of the students and faculty. The library has automated all its library activities.

College has a big playground for outdoor games like Football, Cricket, Kabaddi, Volleyball and Badminton. Races, discus throw, jablin throw, Short put, long jump, high jump and many more athletics programmes.

A well-equipped Hall with seating for over 500 people serves as the venue for the regular conferences, workshops and seminars that are part of the learning process at School Of Management Sciences
